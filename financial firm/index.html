About us

Proelium Finance is an independent corporate finance and private equity firm. We assist our clients with strategic and hands-on advice on all aspects of buy-side and sell-side transactions.

Bull Finance works for both Dutch and international businesses, listed companies, institutional investors and family offices.

Services
(1)
CORPORATE FINANCE
+ +
  • We facilitate a smooth transaction process and support management and other stakeholders in their decision making processes before, during and after the deal. We do this by providing a full suite of advisory and execution services in acquisition and sales processes, from bilateral negotiated deals to controlled auctions. The majority of the deals we are involved in are cross-border. We are well connected and have access to a broad international network.
  • The majority of the deals we are involved in are cross-border. We are well connected and have access to a broad international network.
(2)
PRIVATE EQUITY
+
  • Bull Finance has a distinctive track-record in deal making for and with private equity. We have done numerous deals for private equity funds, family offices and other investors – as we are familiar with their modus operandi.
  • Furthermore, on a case-by-case basis, part of Bull Finance’s remuneration (i.e. the success fee) may be paid in equity. This minimises the cash-out for the acquiring party in the short term and enhances alignment between the parties in the long run.
(3)
MANAGEMENT BUYOUT
+
  • Bull Finance has extensive experience in assisting clients with (secondary) management buyouts. We have a particular focus on assisting management in these processes.
  • Over the years we have successfully initiated, positioned and concluded a large number of management buyouts. Our thorough approach has been a central theme in bringing these complex processes to a successful completion.
(4)
GROWNT FINANCING
+
  • We focus on companies with a clear business model that have already passed the start-up phase. They are profitable, generate cash and have a clear growth strategy.
  • Every investment is assessed on its individual merit. No two entrepreneurs or companies are the same, and our flexible structure and entrepreneurial philosophy give us room to deviate from the above-stated criteria. Consequently, we will also consider companies in the process of full expansion or consolidation with a clear strategy, as well as significant minority interests.
